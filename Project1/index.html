The 2021/2022 Season

The Boston Celtics have already radically reshaped their front office and coaching staffs, traded away a starter who was the last big free agent signing of the team and their first-round draft pick for 2021 while they did it, and the offseason ahead of the 2021-22 NBA season has hardly even started, with two teams still duking it out in the NBA Finals.

But there’s still plenty of work to do and key dates it needs to happen by for the Celtics and new President of Basketball Operations Brad Stevens. There’s enough it can be a little dizzying to the casual fan trying to keep tabs on what their favorite team still needs to do — and when. that end, we put together a list of all the key dates of major decision points remaining in the offseason, as well as other key events that will shape the team’s offseason. Let’s take a look at what still could be on the horizon and the dates on which it would need to occur.
